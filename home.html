PHILIPPINE HISTORY OF THE FIRST PHILIPPINE REPUBLIC

The Malolos Journey: Restoring the Philippine History of the First Philippine Republic interactive museum website is a digital platform dedicated to preserving and pressenting the history of the First Philipine Republic, with a particular focus on the pivotal Malolos Congress of 1898. This online museum seek to make historical knowledge easily accessible and engaging, serving as an educational tool for a global audience.

MUSEUM PROFILE

By digitizing artifacts, documents, and social media content, our website not only contributeto the preservation of cultural heritage but also provides an interactive learning experience. The significance of this initiative lies in its ability to bridge georaphical gaps, fostering community engagement, and utilizing modern technology to ensure the continued appreciation and understanding of historical events that shaped the First Phillipine Republic.
